**Host**: Linux x86_64, cyrius 5.6.25, sit 0.5.1

## sigil SHA-256 throughput

| Input size | Time/op | Implied throughput |
|-----------:|--------:|-------------------:|
| 64 B       | 14 µs   | ~4.6 MB/s |
| 1,024 B    | 113 µs  | ~9.1 MB/s |
| 65,536 B   | 6.286 ms| ~10.4 MB/s |

Throughput levels off around 10 MB/s on large inputs — sit-specific `sit add` on a 1 MB blob is therefore ~100ms+ floor, dominated here. Matches the 11× slower-than-git number in the README.
